## Who we are
AfterCare ("we") is an AI life-navigator app for foster and former-foster youth.

## Our promises
- **We never sell your data.** Ever.
- **We never show ads.**
- **Free forever for foster youth.**
- **You can delete your account and data at any time** (CCPA right to deletion).

## What we collect
- **Account:** phone number, ZIP code, age, foster status, emergency contact.
- **Usage:** chat messages you send (to give you answers and improve the app),
  quest progress, panic events, documents you choose to upload.
- **Device:** a push-notification token (if you opt in), basic analytics events
  (which screens/features are used — never message content or document contents).

## How we use it
- To answer your questions and surface local resources (your ZIP/state are used
  to filter resources to your area).
- To send reminders and opt-in follow-ups (push or SMS).
- To keep the app working and safe.

## AI disclosure
AfterCare is an **AI assistant, not a person**. It is **not** a lawyer or a
therapist. In an emergency, contact 988 or use the Panic button. We do **not**
auto-report to authorities; if you disclose harm, we provide resources.

## How it's stored & protected
- Row-level security isolates your data to your account.
- Documents live in a **private** storage bucket, shared only via short-lived
  links you generate.
- We keep personal identifiers out of AI training/logs.

## Your rights (CCPA/CPRA)
Access, correct, or delete your data, and know what we hold. Contact:

## Children
AfterCare is for youth **14 and older**. We do not knowingly collect data from
children under 13.
